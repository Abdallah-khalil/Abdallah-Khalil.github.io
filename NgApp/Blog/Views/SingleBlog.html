Website users combine random photos to create trippy illusion

 by, Jerry Rose

Wondering how to combine your favorite photograph and your favorite piece of art? Look no further than Russian website Ostagram and Google’s Deep Dream Generator. Ostagram users have been mixing photos and art using the generator, which creates mixed images using a technique called Inceptionism. Inceptionism utilizes neural networks, which “learn” over time by taking in more data, to create these trippy pictures. See some of our favorites below, and other top posts on Ostagram.
